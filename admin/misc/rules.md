## Needs and Expectations
To succeed as a team, we need:
- Communication and consistent updates on progress 
- Support team members who are struggling on assignments
- Accountability from each member to complete their assigned work on time
- Shared understanding of project goals and expectations.
- Just letting each other know on time if a person needs help with something



## Rules and Activities
Our team will use a combination of paired programming, individual work and structured collaboration sessions to complete the project efficiently. 
- Tasks will be divided into pair-based work (for collaboration-heavy parts) and individual work)more focused, independent tasks)
- We will use both synchronous and asynchronous communication depending on availability and task urgency 
- Hold regular standups every Tuesday after class to review progress, set goals, and address blockers. 
- Shared documents will be used to track progress and centralize work.
- Each role/person will have clear responsibilities but collaboration between roles will be encouraged when needed and members can rotate their roles.
